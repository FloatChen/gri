A Cookbook of Gri Recipes

By Dan Kelley, Steve Matheson, Jinyu Shen, Jacquie Witte, ... (c) 1995-1998

This cookbook gives practical examples of Gri programming, with few explanations. To learn more about Gri, see the WWW manual http://www.phys.ocean.dal.ca/~kelley/gri, or the GNUinfo manuals and postscript manuals, at ftp://ftp.phys.ocean.dal.ca/users/kelley/gri

The best recipes are those designed by Gri users. If you have something useful, please contribute.
